How to Play
Volume
Use the in-app slider on the right side to set the alarm volume. Set your device volume to full blast first — the slider then controls the alarm level relative to that.
Changing Blinds
Touch: Swipe right to go up one level, swipe left to go down.
PC: Click the right half of the blinds to go up, left half to go down. Look for the dim + and − signs as guides.
Adjusting the Timer
Touch: Swipe left to add 30 seconds, swipe right to subtract 30 seconds.
PC: Click the right half of the countdown to add 30s, left half to subtract. Won't go below zero or above the level maximum.
Blind Schedule
Levels 1–4 (1&2 through 4&8) run for 15 minutes each. Level 5 (5&10) shows Winding Down to Break — no countdown. Levels 6 and up (Freezeout, starting at 6&12) run for 10 minutes each.
Full Screen
Press F11 to enter full screen. Press F11 again to exit. On a tablet or phone, hold Esc for 3 seconds to return to the menu.
