How to Play
Volume
Alarm volume follows your device's hardware volume buttons — there's no in-app slider. Set it to full blast before you start.
Locking
Tap the padlock to disable swipes and clicks on the blinds and countdown, so they can't be changed by accident. Tap it again to re-enable them — it re-locks itself after 10 seconds of no interaction. Picking a new level from the menu always starts unlocked.
Changing Blinds
Touch: Swipe right to go up one level, swipe left to go down.
PC: Click the right half of the blinds to go up, left half to go down. Look for the dim + and − signs as guides.
Adjusting the Timer
Touch: Swipe left to add 30 seconds, swipe right to subtract 30 seconds.
PC: Click the right half of the countdown to add 30s, left half to subtract. Won't go below zero or above the level maximum.
Blind Schedule
Levels 1–5 (1/2 through 5/10) run for 15 minutes each. Level 6 and up (starting at 6/12) run for 10 minutes each, jumping by +5/+10 from level 16 onward up to 65/130. A 15-minute break starts automatically after level 5 — horn and voice announcement fire at 3 and 1 minutes remaining, then again at zero.
Full Screen
Press F11 to enter full screen. Press F11 again to exit. On a tablet or phone, hold Esc for 3 seconds to return to the menu.
